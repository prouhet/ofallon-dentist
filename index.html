Parking Reality Check

Highway 50 locations can get crowded during peak hours (Tuesday-Thursday 3-6pm). The Shiloh practices typically have better parking availability, and Innovation Dental on Insight Avenue has the most convenient parking setup.

Beat the Rush

Most O'Fallon dental practices are busiest Tuesday-Thursday afternoons and Friday mornings. Try Monday mornings or Tuesday-Thursday 10am-2pm slots for the most attention and shortest wait times.

Timing Your Care

January and September see the biggest new patient promotions, but don't wait if you need care now - most practices will match their best deals year-round if you ask. End-of-year visits maximize insurance benefits.
